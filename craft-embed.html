A Three-Phase Approach That Demands
Commitment And Delivers Results.

The Physique Academy program is built around three core phases. In every phase, the objective is to achieve the fastest possible results without compromising long term sustainability. We move quickly and execute with intent, but time-lines vary by individual. The system stays the same. The speed depends on the person.

01 The Starting Point

Rapid Fat Loss & Foundation

Phase 1 focuses on rapid, controlled fat loss while establishing the foundations for long-term success. Get you to roughly 10–12% body fat, the kind of shape where you look like you belong on the cover of a magazine.

Phase 1 exists to shatter limiting beliefs, prove what consistency can do, and show you what you can achieve when the strategy is correct. This is where results happen fast, executed intelligently and safely.

02 Sustainability And Control

Reverse Diet & Metabolic Reset

This is where fat loss stops being temporary and becomes a permanent part of your lifestyle. Phase 2 prevents rebound weight gain and turns short term progress into a stable, repeatable outcome.

This phase builds confidence around eating, training, and long term bodyweight management. Calories are increased strategically to restore metabolic function, improve energy levels, and maintain the physique achieved in Phase 1.

03 Progression Beyond Fat Loss

Build, Perform & Solidify

This is where fat loss evolves into strength, performance, and long-term physical capability. Build muscle, increase strength, and pursue specific performance goals -physique, endurance, or sport-based, depending on the individual.

Phase 3 solidifies habits, reinforces structure, and establishes a long term standard for training and lifestyle. The outcome is not just improved performance, but the ability to sustain it independently.
